Here's how to play:

You will have 7 chances to guess the Bardle.
For the first three categories, ❌ / ✅ will tell you if you have the incorrect/correct value
For HR, AVG, and WAR, ⬆️ will tell you that the Bardle has a higher value than the player you guessed and ⬇️ will tell you that the Bardle has a lower value than the player you guessed.
✅ means that the player you guessed has the same value as the Bardle.


A few additional things to consider:

The Divsion field is independent of the league.
The WAR is from Baseball Reference.
For players that played for multiple teams in 2021, their team/statistics are just from the last team they played for.
